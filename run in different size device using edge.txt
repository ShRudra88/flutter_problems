Simulate Mobile & Tablet View in Edge
Once the app is running in Edge, follow these steps:

Open DevTools: Press F12 or Ctrl + Shift + I to open Developer Tools.
Toggle Device Toolbar: Click the mobile/tablet icon in the top-left corner of DevTools.
Select a Device: Click the dropdown to choose a device like:
iPhone 14 Pro
Pixel 7
iPad Pro
Galaxy Fold, etc.
Set Screen Size: Manually adjust width & height if needed.
